Soft-Matter for Breakfast

This is a breakfast meeting at NORDITA, Stockholm on the topic of soft-matter . We meet on alternate weeks in the coffee room of NORDITA South building on Monday morning at 9:00. One of us gives a short lecture (about 20 minutes) while the rest tries to remain awake and consume soft matter (typically coffee/tea and cakes).
