Citations, h-index and all that

At present measurement of the productivity and creativity of scientist is an area of research in itself. The focus in on citations, and various indices that can be derived by analyzing citation data. I believe that the citation data is a very limited and ofter wrong measure of a scientist's creativity. What I truly want, is to write papers that will still be read after fifty years. Nevertheless, if you really insist on knowing, you can find the citations of all my paper in my google scholar page. Note that other search engines, e.g., ADS gives somewhat different numbers.
